Who We Are

Pune's Home for
Genuine Motor Oils

AVG AUTO started with one simple belief: every car and bike deserves the best protection. Founded on Porwal Road, Lohegaon, we've grown from a small automotive shop into Pune's most trusted destination for premium motor oils and additives.

As an authorized LIQUI MOLY dealer, we bring Germany's finest motor oil engineering directly to Pune. Every bottle on our shelf is 100% genuine — sourced directly from authorized distributors, never from grey markets or unverified suppliers.

We serve everyone — from the daily commuter who just needs the right oil for their Maruti, to professional mechanics running multi-bay garages who need reliable bulk supply at competitive prices.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2012
🏪 The Beginning
AVG AUTO opens on Porwal Road, Lohegaon. A small automotive accessories shop with big ambitions, serving the local community.
2015
🛢️ LIQUI MOLY Authorization
Became an authorized LIQUI MOLY dealer — one of the first in Lohegaon. Access to Germany's finest motor oils at genuine prices.
2018
📦 500+ Products
Expanded to include Castrol, Shell Helix, Motul, Gulf, Bosch, 3M and more. Became a true one-stop automotive shop.
2022
📱 Online Ordering
Launched on Google Maps and began WhatsApp-based ordering. Customers across Pune could now order from home.
2025
🚀 Full E-Commerce Store
Launched this full Shopify website — serving Pune 24/7 with online ordering, Razorpay UPI payments, and pan-city delivery.
